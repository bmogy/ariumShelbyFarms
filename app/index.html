Welcome home to ARIUM Shelby Farms with the convenience of location and the lifestyle of luxury. Our community offers 1, 2 and 3 bedroom apartment homes that are surrounded by 105 acres of beautiful trees, stocked lakes, and serene walking paths. Our apartment homes boast desirable features including private patios, breakfast bars, open living space and full size washer/dryers. Shelby Farms, a 4500 acre recreational facility, is located right at your back door! Our unsurpassed location provides easy access to I-40, I-240, great shopping and fine dining. Take a swim in one of our resort-style pools or head to our fitness center and cardio studio. Your four-legged family member is welcome at ARIUM Shelby Farms and we accept large dogs!
